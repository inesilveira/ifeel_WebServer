About Us

The Project

ifeel is a platform for anonymous, creative, self-expression which acts as a safe space for people to destress. This platform allows users to anonymously dump their daily stress and emotions into a collaborative feed. With the use of prompts, users can direct their emotions and create expressive results. We want you to feel like you are part of something bigger when you submit. ifeel - let’s feel together!

We also have an extension to our web application - the ifeel shop. There you can customize merchandise using your expressive results. Additionally, we have cool merchandise with our brand that you can browse and shop.

Our Team

We are a team of three university students: Pedro Miguel, Inês Silveira, and Mariya Nyzhnikova. This was a project for the 2nd year project class of the Creative Technologies course at IADE.
